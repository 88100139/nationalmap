Frequently Asked Questions

What is the National Map?

The National Map is a web based application developed by the National ICT Australia as part of an initiative of Department of Communications and working closely with Geoscience Australia. It is developed on top of existing open source software Cesium and Leaflet with WebGL and Canvas support.

Read more about it on the National Map About page

How do I report bugs or suggestions for NationalMap?

There are three ways to contact us:

  • During GovHack, you can reach us via the @NICTA twitter account, using #NationalMap and/or #NMsupport hastags.
  • We have a github repo for you to list any issues or bugs you find.
  • Email us on nationalmap@lists.nicta.com.au

We are also keen to hear any ideas or suggestions that may not already be in our release notes.
